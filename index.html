About Us

Sutakami library was established in 2002 to promote the availability of general knowledge. This library was built and donated by Mr. Graham Millington and Mr. Diana Millington from England. This library is 50 feet in length and 40 feet in width and is divided into three rooms: a computer room, a reading room and a general room. There are 12,700 books in the Sutakami Library and the books are classified by category such as young learner, Junior Fiction, Fiction, Non-fiction, Reference books, Newspapers, Magazine and Burmese books. Sutakami library provides library card valid for six months per person and lend three books out per week.
